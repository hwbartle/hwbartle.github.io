4. Cookies

Beruff & Company may use browser cookies on the Site to keep track of your visit to our Site. A "cookie" is a small amount of data that is sent from a Web server to your browser and stored on your computer's hard drive. Use of cookies may allow Beruff & Company to provide a higher quality, more relevant, customer experience. We may also use cookies to track which areas of the web site are most popular with our users and to better understand overall use of the site. With most browsers, you can erase cookies from your computer hard drive, block all cookies, or receive a warning before a cookie is stored on your computer. If you elect to block cookies, you may not be able to access certain features of the Site. Please refer to your browser instructions or a help screen to learn more about these functions and how to use them.
