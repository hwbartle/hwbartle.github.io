Management Consulting

Beruff & Company partners with companies and investors to provide consulting services in eight critical areas of performance. Our goal is to help create lasting value for our clients, which leads to a sustainable competitive advantage in the marketplace for talent, customers and capital. Our strategies are based on actual experience as business leaders and are structured to be practicable for our clients. We act with speed, reducing time to innovation and performance improvement. Our consulting services encompass the following areas:

  • Strategic Guidance: Assisting with strategy formulation to create defensible competitive advantages and positions of leadership
  • Marketing Strategy: Optimizing product positioning, branding, focus, value proposition, and differentiation
  • New Product Development: Evaluating the new product pipeline to set priorities
  • Business Development: Structuring, and negotiating, commercial and technology agreements
  • Organization: Helping to build more effective leadership teams energized by a meaningful mission, aspirational goals and higher standards
  • Operating Leverage: Enhancing operational performance through improved scalability, margins, and cash flow
  • Financial Strategy: Optimizing cash flow and capital structure
  • Investments: Evaluating investment opportunities and providing strategic transaction support
